ThreeDee

An innovative way to order 3d prints

What is this?

ThreeDee is a website that makes ordering a 3d print super easy.

Why should I use this?

If you order a print through this website, you will often get your print faster, it will be easier to customize, and you can browse a large collection of items all in one place. Also, you can use the site currency, credits, to ease the whole proccess and potentially get prints cheaper.

What are credits?

Credits are a form of currency you can use on the site to buy 3d prints. When you give $1 to me I give you 10 credits. If you pay with credits, you get a discount and you can get your order finished faster. Also, there are many opportunities to get free credits to use.

Tell me about your 3d printer!

I have the Solidoodle 2, which lets me print in ABS, PLA, wood, or nylon, among other materials. It can print objects up to 6 inches x 6 inches x 6 inches. I am using a heated glass surface to print on, which greatly eases the print proccess and allows for the highest quality prints.

Can I get stuff custom designed and printed?

Probably, but not yet through this website. Talk to me in person if you want something designed.
